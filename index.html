Who We Are

A Passion for Storytelling

The Media Unit is a student-led organization dedicated to chronicling the life and spirit of our college. We are photographers, videographers, writers, and designers who believe in the power of media to connect and inspire our community.

From thrilling sports events to prestigious academic ceremonies, we provide comprehensive coverage to ensure no significant moment is forgotten.
